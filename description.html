The project "Echo" is an interactive comic that explores the boundaries between reality and imagination through a girl's journey. Echo, the protagonist, enters an art exhibition, and her imaginative mind takes her on a surreal adventure. Dreamlike natural landscapes, from forests to oceans, unfold before her, creating an "echo" between the physical external exhibition and her vivid inner world. This interactive format and the animations allow the reader to experience Echo's fluid transitions between reality and fantasy and her abundant spiritual world.
